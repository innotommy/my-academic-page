I am a fifth-year PhD student advised by Engin Kirda, working as a member of the Secure Systems Lab (SecLab) at Northeastern University.
Previously, I studied in Politecnico di Milano, where I got my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Computer Science Engineering. I also studied at Universidad Complutense de Madrid during this period under the Erasmus exchange program.
Interests. Web security and privacy
